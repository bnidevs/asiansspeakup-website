What's This?

Write a story! Anything that dwells upon your past and your Asian heritage that brought you to where you are today. It could be a happy, painful, or life changing experience; any type of story you want! The goal is to show that with all these stories combined, we create a powerful community that can combat all the hate and physical pain towards Asians. So, what are you waiting for? Fill out the form on our website or send it directly to us through Instagram. If you are uncomfortable with publishing your name but still want to share your experience, there is an anonymous option on our website for quotes or shorter stories!

P.S. If you choose to submit a story, we will get in touch for a picture!
